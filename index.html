Frequently Asked Questions

How to prepare items for sale?

Clean and sort your materials by type. Remove any non-recyclable components like plastic labels from metal items or batteries from electronics.

Do you accept broken electronics?

Yes, we accept broken electronics but the price may be lower than working items. Please remove any batteries before bringing them in.

What are your business hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Is there a minimum weight requirement?

No minimum weight, but items under 1kg may be paid at a slightly lower rate due to handling costs.

Recycling Tips

Proper Sorting

Separate materials by type (metals, plastics, glass) to maximize their recycling potential and get better prices.

Clean Materials

Rinse containers and remove food residue. Clean materials are worth more and are easier to process.

Compact When Possible

Flatten boxes, crush cans, and cut large items to save space and reduce transportation costs.

Battery Safety

Remove batteries from electronics and recycle them separately to prevent fires and contamination.

Remove Labels

Peel off non-recyclable components like plastic labels from glass jars and metal cans.

Check Local Rates

Prices fluctuate - call ahead or check our price trends before bringing in large quantities.

Pro Recycling Tips

  • Copper wire with insulation removed fetches 30% higher prices
  • Keep aluminum separate from other metals for better pricing
  • Electronics with intact components may be worth more as parts than scrap
  • Separate colored glass from clear glass - they have different recycling streams
  • Bundle similar items together to save time during weighing
